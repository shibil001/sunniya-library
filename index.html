SAC LIBRARY

Sunniyya Arabic College is situated at Chennamangallur village located in Kozhikode district. It was established in 1980 and got affiliation in 1995. The college has a congenial atmosphere for academic pursuint and aims at providing education to the local people. The village Chennamangallur, which is situated 25kms East of the district head quarters, has a dense population of minority community.

Our Campus

The Sunni Education Trust, which runs the institution, aims at the uplift of educationally backward classes. The college spreads over an area of 5.5 acres at the bank of the river 'Eruvazhinhi' and it provides a serene atmosphere for the students and faculty.
